About Awo Data Science Consulting

Awo Data Science Consulting is a boutique advisory firm specialising in data strategy, analytics, and results-based management. We operate at the intersection of public sector reform, development programming, and modern data science.

We understand the realities of working with incomplete data, evolving institutions, and complex stakeholder environments in fragile and emerging contexts. Our work combines strong technical skills with deep practical experience in programme management, governance, and institutional strengthening in Somalia and the region.

What We Deliver

  • Data strategies aligned with national plans and sector policies
  • Executive-ready dashboards and KPI frameworks for reforms
  • Forecasting and risk analysis to support medium-term planning
  • M&E systems that connect indicators to real data sources

How We Work

  • Start from the decision, not from the tool or buzzword
  • Design solutions that can be owned and maintained locally
  • Translate technical analysis into clear, actionable messages
  • Build capacity of national teams, not dependency on consultants
